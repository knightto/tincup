Tin Cup 2026 Admin Guide

This page documents the current Tin Cup admin workflow: where data is entered, which items are manual, and which pages are verification-only.

Page Map

Tin Cup 2026 Main trip page. Use it to review the schedule, open Trip Info, jump to Score, Results, Lodging, and add trip tee times to your calendar.
Score Main admin workspace. Start in Leader Inputs for roster, scoring config, and manual side-game setup, then use Score-Driven Entry for live hole scores, scramble scoring, markers, and scorecard access.
Results Read-only verification page. Use it to confirm totals, gross and net stroke results, match points, scramble points, skins, side games, payouts, the readable report, and audit rows after entries are saved.
Lodging Manual condo, room, extra-night, and payment management. The golfer list on this page comes from the Admin roster and should not be edited here.

1. Set Up The Outing

  1. Open Admin first. Complete the leader-input section before anyone starts scoring.
  2. Use Golfer Roster Setup to enter the player list, handicaps, and classification tags such as previous champ or rookie.
  3. Use Scoring Rules and Payout Config to confirm entry fee, place payouts, side-game values, skins value, and scramble point settings.
  4. Use Manual Side Game Inputs to confirm Long Putt and any other leader-entered winners that are not derived directly from golf scores.
  5. Use Admin Tools to confirm live scoring, live markers, leaderboard access, and reset/reseed controls before Round 1 starts.
  6. Return to the trip page and confirm the top links open the correct Tin Cup scoring and results pages.

2. Test The Outing

  1. Use Clear And Reseed Demo Scores on Admin if you want a full practice run with fresh sample results.
  2. Open at least one live scorecard and confirm hole entry works, current-hole navigation works, and the full scorecard can be shown and hidden.
  3. Open a scramble team and enter a few scramble holes to confirm scramble totals and points update.
  4. Enter a marker on a valid Closest To Pin or Long Drive hole and confirm it appears on the results page.
  5. Open Results and confirm the leaderboard, stroke results, scramble, side games, readable report, and payouts all render without missing data.

3. Confirm Scoring Accuracy

  1. Use Stroke Results (Read-Only) on Results to compare gross and net numbers for Day 1, Day 3, and Day 4.
  2. Use Tin Cup Match Scoring By Day and Hole-By-Hole Match Detail to verify match points came from the entered hole scores.
  3. Use Scramble Results to verify team totals, rank, and distributed scramble points.
  4. Use Skins, Manual Side Game Inputs, and Payout Summary to verify side-game winners and money distribution.
  5. Use Workbook Results Audit as the final cross-check table to confirm trip finish, score ranking, scramble, skins, and payouts all agree.

4. Save The Report

  1. Open Results and expand Readable Final Report.
  2. Select Generate Readable Report to build the presentation-friendly winner summary.
  3. Select Print / Save As PDF to save a readable final report for the group leader.
  4. Use Open Final Competition Report or Open Printable Report from Admin when you need a polished presentation document for the group leader.
  5. Use Export Final Competition CSV from Admin when you also want a raw archive of standings, scorecards, scramble, markers, side games, and payouts.

Main Trip Page

  • Use Trip Info to open the travel and trip details modal.
  • Use the calendar to switch days inside the current Tin Cup trip month. The calendar is locked to the trip month and does not move outside it.
  • Use Add trip tee times to your calendar to download the trip tee times into a calendar app.
  • Use the top chips to open Results, Lodging, Score, and this guide.
  • Event cards, tee times, travel links, map links, and course details are primarily review surfaces on this page, not the main place for scoring changes.

Live Scorecard Access

  • Choose a round from the Day selector. This includes Scramble in the same selector as the other rounds.
  • Open a group link to launch the live scorecard for a foursome or scramble team.
  • Scorers enter their name when opening a card. The page is organized to keep the current hole and relevant marker visible without showing the entire scorecard by default.
  • Use the hole jump strip to move between holes. The mobile score pad supports -, +, Clear, and quick score chips.
  • Use Full Scorecard only for review. Hole-by-hole entry is the main scoring surface.
  • On non-scramble rounds, the scorecard auto-locks as soon as all 18 holes are entered for every player. Editing a submitted card still requires an admin-code override.

Markers And Side Games

  • Live Markers is where Closest To Pin and Long Drive winners are assigned on applicable holes.
  • Manual Side Game Inputs is where Long Putt and other leader-entered side-game winners are saved.
  • Secret Snowman is score-driven and is drawn from an eligible score of 8 after the relevant day has real or seeded scores.
  • The leaderboard and payout tables update from the saved Long Putt selection and the Secret Snowman draw result.
  • If marker entry is hidden, re-enable it from the Admin Tools live-scoring toggles.

Golfer Roster Setup

  • Use Add Golfer Row to insert a blank player row.
  • Use Import Golfers CSV to load roster rows from a CSV file.
  • Use Save Golfer Setup after roster edits.
  • Each row includes Name, Handicap, Classification, and Penalty.
  • Classification is where previous champions and rookies are identified. Penalty is where manual scoring penalties are assigned to a golfer.
  • This roster is the source for the Lodging page. Do not try to add golfers from Lodging.

Scoring Rules And Payout Config

These values drive handicap conversion, points, side-game payouts, scramble points, and the final payout model.

Handicap fields Index->HC %, Avg Course Slope, Avg Slope, Max HC, and Par 3 Fraction.
Trip pot fields Entry Fee, Winner, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th.
Side-game payouts Long Drive, CTP, Long Putt, Secret Snowman, Loser, and Skins / Person.
Scramble points Scramble 1st, Scramble 2nd, and Scramble 3rd.
Save action Use Save Scoring Config after changes. The surrounding Pot Summary and handicap verification table are there to confirm the numbers before and after saving.

Admin Tools

  • Live Foursome Scoring: turns access to the Score live entry flow on or off.
  • Closest To Pin / Long Drive Entry: shows or hides marker entry on the scoring page.
  • Live Leaderboard Page: shows or hides the dedicated live results page and nav chip.
  • Admin Code is used for protected admin actions, including reseeding, reset, and submitted-card override edits.
  • Clear And Reseed Demo Scores removes existing competition scoring and generates a fresh random demo event for testing every scoring surface.
  • Clear Competition removes scorecards, scramble scores, markers, side-game winners, and penalties so Tin Cup is ready for Round 1 while keeping player names, handicaps, live settings, and scoring config.
  • Open Final Competition Report opens the polished summary document built from the final standings, score proof, side games, and payouts.
  • Open Printable Report opens that same document in print-friendly mode so it can be saved as PDF for the group.
  • Export Final Competition CSV downloads a full raw archive of final standings, scorecards, scramble results, markers, skins, side games, and payouts.

Lodging Page

  • Use the status summary and condo groups to see who is assigned, unassigned, or outside the active condo count.
  • Manual lodging inputs include Assign to condo, Move condo, Room, Unassign, Extra night, and Paid.
  • The Lodging page is also where Tin Cup payment amounts are maintained.
  • The roster list itself is not edited here. If a golfer is missing or needs to be removed, do that in Admin.

Results Page

  • Use Day to switch the live day leaderboard.
  • Use the match day tabs to review hole-by-hole match results by day.
  • Review read-only sections for readable final report, stroke results, score rankings, skins, scramble, side games, payouts, and workbook results audit.
  • Stroke Results (Read-Only) now shows both gross and net round totals for scoring confirmation.
  • If numbers look wrong here, the fix normally belongs on the Admin, Score, or Lodging page, not on the results page itself.

Automatic Vs Manual

Automatic calculations: gross and net day totals, trip totals, handicap stroke application, penalties, Secret Snowman draw eligibility, skins, scramble points, payout rollups, readable report output, and audit tables after data is saved.
  • Manual entry surfaces: roster rows, handicaps, classifications, penalties, hole scores, markers, Long Putt, live-access toggles, condo assignments, extra nights, and payment amounts.
  • Verification surfaces: Results, Readable Final Report, Pot Summary, handicap verification, full scorecard review, and trip-page schedule/event displays.

Recommended Admin Workflow

  1. Before the trip, finish every field in Leader Inputs: roster, handicap values, classifications, penalties, payout config, scramble points, and any manual side-game setup.
  2. Before each round, confirm the correct day is selected, live scoring is enabled, and the scorecard links open the expected foursomes or scramble teams.
  3. During play, enter hole scores first. Let the scorecards drive every value that can be derived from actual golf scores.
  4. After score entry, save markers and any manual side-game winners that are not score-driven.
  5. After each round, review Results using gross/net stroke results, match detail, scramble, skins, payouts, and workbook audit to confirm the math.
  6. At the end of the event, generate the Readable Final Report, save it as PDF, and export the final CSV archive.
